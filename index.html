PROJECT (i)

AlertGen — Full Product

AlertGen

Sole Designer: Concept, Prototype, and Logic

The Challenge: Designing an interface that handles high-density data (ingredient lists, air quality metrics, and skin analysis) without overwhelming a user who might be in a state of high stress or urgency.

The Solution: I developed a "Clarity-First" design system that prioritizes immediate action. The UI is centered around a modular dashboard that adapts based on the user's personal profile, highlighting only the allergens relevant to them.

Adaptive Scanning UI: Designed an OCR overlay that provides real-time visual feedback, highlighting "Safe" vs "Danger" ingredients directly on the camera feed.

Accessibility & Speed: Engineered one-tap and long-press widgets to bypass the home screen, ensuring that life-saving tools like the Emergency Contact Alert and Digital First Aid Guide are reachable in under two seconds.

PROJECT (ii)

Exler — Excercises and Licensure Exam reviewers

Exler

Designer, UX, Visuals

The Challenge: Traditional review centers burden students with high costs and rigid, location-bound schedules, while specialized lecturers lack a unified platform to market their niche expertise directly to those who need it.

The Solution: A decentralized, virtual-first educational ecosystem. The platform bridges this gap by offering affordable, tiered subscriptions for students while providing freelancers a scalable space to monetize module-specific materials and video lectures.

Specialized Educator Hub: Designed a streamlined dashboard for lecturers to effortlessly manage virtual meetings, post announcements, and distribute resources without the overhead of traditional center management.

Accessible & Interactive Learning: Structured the student interface for ultimate flexibility, integrating on-demand video lectures, downloadable offline resources, and AI-assisted review tools to support self-paced, 24/7 learning.

PROJECT (iii)

Recyclaim — Waste Management App

Recyclaim

2 Designers, UX, Visuals, 3D

The Challenge: Encouraging consistent recycling habits among everyday consumers by overcoming common barriers like a lack of accessibility to drop-off points and the absence of tangible incentives.

The Solution: We created an engaging, eco-focused platform that gamifies the recycling process, connecting users to local facilities and rewarding their sustainable actions with a seamless digital coin economy.

Location-Based Discovery: Integrated an intuitive mapping system to help users effortlessly locate nearby participating recycling centers and drop-off points with ease.

Frictionless Reward Ecosystem: Implemented a fast QR code scanning feature that instantly credits users with reward coins for their recyclables, which can be exchanged for discounts and cashback offers.

PROJECT (iv)

Track in Field — Farm monitoring app

Track in Field

UI/UX, Visuals

The Challenge: Bridging the gap in traditional farm management by providing a unified digital tool that addresses the complexities of tracking crops, managing finances, and facilitating communication between landowners and agricultural laborers.

The Solution: Designed an intuitive, all-in-one platform that fosters sustainable farming and drives productivity through real-time data tracking, expense management, and seamless community collaboration.

Comprehensive Farm Management: Delivered robust tools for detailed crop planning to monitor planting schedules and growth stages, alongside a categorized system for tracking real-time agricultural expenses.

Enhanced Agricultural Collaboration: Built dedicated communication and task management features that empower landowners and laborers to easily share tasks, notes, and updates to ensure efficient daily operations.
